Club

Penn State Car Club

The Penn State Car Club is an organization for all things that move on wheels! We are interested in cars, trucks, motorcycles, and anything in between! We are more of a social-oriented club that hosts many different types of vehicle-oriented events like car meets, socials, track days, dyno days, drag days, and many more! We meet once or twice a week to come together and talk about our passion while surrounded by the vehicles that we drive.



International Student Council

the International Student Council (ISC) serves as a representative organization for all international student communities. Whether at the University Park campus or any of the 19 Commonwealth locations, the ISC recognizes, respects, and represents international students. They provide support, organize events, and foster a sense of community.
